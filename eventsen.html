Basura — A New Musical

📍 Alliance Theatre — Coca-Cola Stage, Atlanta

The world premiere of the musical based on the true story of Paraguay's Recycled Orchestra of Cateura. With music and lyrics by Gloria and Emily Estefan, and directed by Michael Greif (Rent, Dear Evan Hansen).

CPAG will organize a group outing to see the show together. Pre-show tereré included. Details will be announced in the WhatsApp group.
